# healtcare_lifestyle
The primary goal of this project is to analyze and monitor the relationship between lifestyle habits and health outcomes using an interactive dashboard built in Microsoft Power BI. It helps organizations and individuals identify patterns in sleep, stress, BMI, and physical activity to improve overall well-being and reduce health risks.

Business Problem

Modern lifestyles—especially in corporate environments—are leading to:

Poor sleep quality
Increased stress levels
Rising cases of obesity and high blood pressure
Reduced physical activity

Organizations lack a centralized system to monitor these health indicators and derive actionable insights.

💡 Solution

This dashboard provides a data-driven solution by consolidating health-related data and presenting it through interactive visualizations. It enables users to:

Track key health metrics in real time
Identify high-risk individuals or groups
Understand how lifestyle factors (sleep, stress, occupation) impact health

** Key Features & Functionalities**
Executive Overview: High-level KPIs like average sleep, total people, and risk categories
Sleep Analysis: Sleep duration and quality across age groups and occupations
Behavioral Insights: Relationship between stress level, heart rate, and sleep disorders
BMI & Risk Segmentation: Categorization into Low, Medium, and High-risk groups
Activity Tracking: Correlation between physical activity and sleep quality
Interactive Filters: Drill-down by Sleep Disorder, Stress Level, and Occupation

Key Insights Generated

Identification of occupations with poor sleep quality
Correlation between high stress and sleep disorders (e.g., insomnia)
Impact of BMI on daily activity and health risks
Patterns showing how physical activity improves sleep quality

** Business Impact**

Helps organizations design employee wellness programs
Enables early detection of high-risk health conditions
Supports data-driven decision-making in healthcare and HR
Improves productivity by addressing sleep and stress-related issues

Technology Used

Microsoft Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
Data Modeling & DAX for calculations
